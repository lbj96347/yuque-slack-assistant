Introduction
============

It's an assistant app for Yuque in Slack. 

How to Use
===========

*** Config *** 

Replace `SLACK_WEBHOOK` in `config.temp.py` , you can check it out from your Slack App Setting. 

*** Develop ***

> git clone 

> virtualenv env 

> 

*** Deploy ***

> start: make start-pro

> stop: make stop-pro
